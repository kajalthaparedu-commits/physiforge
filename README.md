## 🌌 About PhysiForge

PhysiForge is an interactive physics universe designed to help students explore physical laws through simulations, visualisations, and experiments.

The platform is designed for modern physics education and aims to go beyond traditional simulation tools by creating an **immersive physics exploration environment.**

---

## ⚡ First Simulation

### Electric Field Football

Students place electric charges to steer a football using electric fields.

Concepts explored:

• Coulomb's Law  
• Electric Field Vectors  
• Superposition of Fields  
• Force on a Test Charge  
• Field Line Visualisation
